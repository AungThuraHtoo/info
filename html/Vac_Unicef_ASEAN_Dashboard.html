Vaccine Coverage ASEAN Dashboard

These dashboards provide insights on the coverage of 14 different vaccines and comparisons among 10 ASEAN countries. The audience can choose vaccine type, country, and specific year. The second dashboard highlights vaccine coverage trends from 2000 to the latest year. Data source: UNICEF immunization datasets.
